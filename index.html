    • Pokemon Predictions

    This project is intended to see how Pokemon statistics correlate to various other attributes, and see how likely a pokemon's stats can be predicted by those attributes.

    There are 6 Pokemon stats: HP (health points), Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed.

    I looked to see if these stats could be infered by looking at a Pokemon's weight, height, or type.

    I also mapped out how these stats change over the generations of Pokemon games.

    I was also interested to see how different types of Pokemon are colored, and how legendary, mythical, and form-changing Pokemon are distributed across generations 1-8.
